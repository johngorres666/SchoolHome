Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (PLM) is a public university in Manila, known for its affordable yet high-quality education.

Universidad de Manila

Universidad de Manila (UdM) offers accessible education to Manila residents, excelling in public administration and criminology.

Lyceum of the Philippines University

Lyceum of the Philippines University (LPU) is known for its programs in Business, Law, and Engineering.

Mapúa University

Mapúa University is a top institution for STEM education, offering strong programs in engineering, architecture, and IT.

University of Santo Tomas

Founded in 1611, UST is one of Asia's oldest universities, offering a wide range of programs with a strong Catholic tradition.

Technological University of the Philippines

Technological University of the Philippines (TUP) is a leading public university specializing in engineering, technology, and applied sciences. Founded in 1901, it focuses on producing skilled professionals ready for industry challenges.
